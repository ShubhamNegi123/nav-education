Mari State Medical
University

Mari State University was founded in 1972 and it is considered to be a classical university of Mari El Republic. The University is a member of the Russian Association of Classical Universities, the member of the Volga Region’s Association of classical universities, and the establisher of the Association of Finno-Ugric Universities. This University is one of the most effective multi-field universities of the Volga Federal District and Russia. Mari State University plays an important role in the political, socio-economic, and spiritual development of the country as a whole.
The University campus has 7 educational buildings and 8 halls of residence. The university has a huge campus with a total area of 1,28,000 square meters. There are 400 candidates of sciences and associate lecturers and there is a staff of about 100 doctors of sciences and professors.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN RUSSIA FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • The quality of education in Mari State University is excellent and thus it is comparable to the European standards.
  • Mari State University follows the international curriculum.
  • The classrooms and laboratories in Mari State Medical University are well equipped and of high quality with all the modern infrastructure and technology.
  • Mari State Medical University is listed in the world’s major medical organizations like World Health Organization, National Medical Commission, European Medical Council, UNESCO and the Medical Councils of many other countries and also it is considered to be the center of education and scientific research.
  • There is an effective feedback system in Mari State University which helps the university authorities to take into account the students’ opinions about important decision-making.
  • No placement test, or No IELTS/TOFEL is required for admission into Mari State University.
  • The university is recognized by Who, NMC, USMLE, GMC and is listed in FAIMER list.
  • There is no donation required for admission into this university.
  • Low educational cost and low living expense for studying MBBS at this university.
  • The city where the university is located has a cold and temperate climate throughout the year.
  • The Mari State Medical University has one of the largest research centers of Mari EL republic, where natural science and humanitarian based research are held.
