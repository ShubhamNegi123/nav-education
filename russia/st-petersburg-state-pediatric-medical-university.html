St. Petersbug State Pediatric
University

ST. Petersburg State Pediatric Medical University has been an international centre for higher medical education for global students striving to pursue a medical career. ST. Petersburg State Pediatric Medical University was founded on January 7, 1925, and situated in ST. Petersburg, Russia.
The institute has 3700 students trained in 66 departments under 810 skilled and highly qualified teachers. 29000 students have recently graduated from ST. Petersburg State Pediatric Medical University. Every year thousands of foreign students coming from 70 countries and 4 continents graduate from ST. Petersburg State Pediatric Medical University.
The doors of the ST. Petersburg State Pediatric Medical University are open for persons of any gender, nation and race, regardless of political and religious beliefs, from any state. St. Petersburg State Pediatric University provides a high standard of the education system. Today ST. Petersburg State Pediatric Medical University has more than 60 departments, more than 15 thousand modern operations and almost 100 thousand satisfied, and healthy babies from all over Russia per year.
