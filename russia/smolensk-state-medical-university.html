Smolensk State Medical
University

Smolensk State Medical University is a government medical school established in 1920. Dr. R.S.Kozlov is the chancellor of the university & member of the American Society for Microbiology and the European Respiratory Society.
MBBS at Smolensk State Medical University is accepted worldwide, therefore, the students from 50 countries like India, Germany, Italy, Poland and other countries of African, Asian and South America continents come to study MBBS in Smolensk State Medical University.
The university trains the medical and pharmaceutical specialists of overseas countries. Students at the university can work in renowned teaching hospitals with updated equipment to diagnose and treat various diseases.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN RUSSIA FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • Cost of living at Smolensk State Medical University is low.
  • Cost of the MBBS study is low.
  • The university provides clinical exposure to international students.
  • The university organizes student exchange programs with various healthcare establishments in the EU.
  • Students are trained in scientific and clinical research during the course of study.
  • It is not obligatory to pass any entrance examination while enrolling for the MBBS course except NEET exams.
