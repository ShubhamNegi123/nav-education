Rostov State Medical University was established in 1930. The university is the best choice for medical aspirants to pursue MBBS in Russia, due to its quality education which is approved by the ministry on health and social medicine. Rostov State Medical University is among the top medical universities located in the city of Rostov-on-Don.
In the Rostov State Medical University every year the intake takes place in the month of September. Students are advised to fill the admission forms before 15th July.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN RUSSIA FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • Rostov State Medical University offers a low-cost fee structure which makes it very much affordable.
  • There is a Centre scientific research laboratory in Rostov State Medical University, which is best for doing MBBS in Russia.
  • The Rostov State Medical University is having the best teaching staff consisting of both scientists and medical practitioners.
  • The University is having International cooperation with many nations.
  • The Rostov State Medical University has the largest training facility in Russia.
  • The medium of teaching the MBBS course in English and Russian language.
  • The Rostov State Medical University is having well-developed infrastructure.
