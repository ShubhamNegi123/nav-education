Belgorod State
University

Belgorod State National Research University is located in the city of Belgorod to the west of River Volga and it was founded in 1876. It has gone through various transformations before evolving as a renowned name in the field of medical education and today it is one of the leading universities in Russia.
Currently, Belgorod State National Research University trains more than 30,000 students in various programs including MBBS in Russia.
Belgorod State University provides the Best education to 30000 students with an extensive academic structure of 20 faculties and 1 institute. The university provides an extensive range of Bachelor’s and Master’s Programmes in English with excellent teaching methodology and well-resourced teaching equipment along with a remarkable education Curriculum.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN RUSSIA FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • Belgorod State University is a one among the top medical universities located in Russia.
  • The course is taught in English at Belgorod State University, and thus a large number of international students including Indian students currently pursue MBBS in Russia.
  • The medical degree which students get after completing the MBBS course in this university is recognized globally.
  • The Belgorod State University has a long history and is one of the oldest universities in Russia.
  • Students studying at Belgorod State National Research University can take advantage of student exchange programs as the university has tie-ups with many American and European universities.
  • Students are not required to qualify IELTS/TOEFL screening tests to study at this university.
  • The technologies and methodologies used at this university are always updated as this is a growing institution.
  • On-campus accommodation facilities are available for foreign students at Belgorod State National Research University.
  • There is no entrance exam to study MBBS at this university.
  • At leading hospitals of the Belgorod region, clinical practices are conducted.
