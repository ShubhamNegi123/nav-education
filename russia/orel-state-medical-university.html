Orel State Medical
University

Orel State University is one of the leading medical institutes in Russia established in 1931 and was renamed on September 11, 1996, from Pedagogical institute to Orel State University. The MBBS course taught at Orel State University is considered as one of the top MBBS course in Russia. Today, the university has implemented the 110 educational programs in 14 different areas of training and specialties.
Orel State University is the top medical college in Russia that fabricates physicists, chemists, biologists, mathematicians and medical professionals in the leading areas of medical science and economy.
At present, the university has 17 departments including 100+ teachers, 30 professors, 77 associate professors,12 academicians, 8 honored doctors of Russian Federation, 11 members of various public academies. Clinical classes of the institute are conducted in Regional hospital, Children’s Regional Hospital, City Emergency Hospital, Venereal Diseases Hospital in order to make the students more familiar with their patients.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN RUSSIA FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • Orel State University offers holistic exposure to students in areas of medicine.
  • The students will get an opportunity to work on different channels of the university that engages the students with research programs, training programs and in different specialized departments of the university.
  • Relatively, the university has a low cost of education which attracts the number of Indian students each year simultaneously.
  • No capitations or donation fees will be charged from the students since admissions are completely on merit basis.
  • A medical degree from Orel State University is globally accepted.
  • Excellent quality of education is provided by the experienced staff and renowned faculty of the university.
  • University has a student’s welfare association which ensures the need for students that redefines the method of learning and teaching.
