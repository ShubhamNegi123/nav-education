Orenburg State Medical
University

Orenburg State Medical University was founded by F.M. Lazarenko in August 1944 as Chkalov Medical Institute. On July 15, 1994, the Institute was reorganized into ‘The Orenburg State Medical University’, according to the decision of the State committee of Russia on Higher School.
Orenburg State Medical University is a small, non-profit co-educational higher education institution which has a student enrollment range of 3,000-3,999 students and around 555 members of scientific staff.
An efficient system of continuous multilevel training of specialists with superior pharmaceutical and medical education can be seen in the Orenburg State Medical University. For pursuing MBBS and many other medical degrees, Orenburg State Medical University is ranked among the top medical universities of Russia.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN RUSSIA FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • The cost of studying MBBS at Orenburg State Medical University is very affordable compared to other countries.
  • The University provides quality education which is comparable to many developed countries like the USA, UK and European countries.
  • The students who take their MBBS degree from Orenburg State Medical University are eligible to work in any part of the world after passing the national medical exam of that country.
  • The University is recognized by the Ministry of Health & Education of Russia.
  • The students get a very healthy environment with all the facilities and world-class infrastructure.
  • The university gives hands-on training and practical clinical rounds to students which help them in their overall growth.
  • The big advantage for international students is that the medium of instruction is English.
  • All the facilities like libraries, the Internet, personal hygiene rooms, sports areas, and household rooms with all the necessary equipment are provided to the students at Orenburg State Medical University.
