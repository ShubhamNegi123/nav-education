Kazan Federal Medical
University

Founded in 1804, Kazan (Volga region) Federal University has become the second oldest university of Russia.
It has successfully completed more than 200 years. In 2010 the university got its Federal status. It started offering MBBS courses from 2013 under the Kazan Federal University Institute of Fundamental Medicine and Biology. Now it is the main educational centre of Tatarstan.
According to the QS ranking system, the Kazan Federal University comes under the world’s top 400 universities.
It has associations with over 10 clinics and hospitals to get the students the practice that is necessary during their clinical postings.The University is undoubtedly a good destination for future doctors and Indian students. Availability of Indian food at the walking distance from university is one of the reasons for that.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN RUSSIA FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • It is the second oldest university of Russia.
  • There are 415 laboratories and university clinics available for students.
  • Number of international students: over 11,000 from 101 countries.
  • 8,82,000 m2 infrastructure of the university.
  • It is also one of the 15 Russian universities which were selected to participate in 5–100 Russian Academic Excellence Project coordinated by the Government of the Russian Federation.
  • The Kazan Federal University consists of nearly 3000 professors. All of them are expert in their subjects and knowledgeable. Most of them hold doctoral degrees and they belong to not only from Russia but also from the rest of the globe.
  • They not only teach students but also guide them which will empower the graduates to become leaders in industry and academia.
  • Students can work anywhere in the world once they complete their MBBS from Kazan Federal University is included in the World Directory of Medical schools and NMC.
