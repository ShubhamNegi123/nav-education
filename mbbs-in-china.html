ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN CHINA FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • All medical schools are listed in World Health Organization (WHO) and it means that the graduates from these medical schools or universities can attend national medical screening tests such as MCI, PMDC, USMLE, PLAB, SCHS etc.
  • The medical degrees obtained from the medical universities in China are recognized globally.
  • China's medical universities are ranked among the top 500 universities in the world.
  • It is easy to get admission in a top medical university in China than USA, UK, or Europe, even India or Pakistan.
  • Studying MBBS in China is 70% cheaper than studying it in US or UK. In China, the duration of MBBS program is of 6 years. It is very affordable for international students to study in China.
  • Students can experience various international cultures under one roof.
