Zaporozhye State Medical
University

Zaporozhye State Medical University is one of the multidisciplinary institutions in Ukraine having the 4th level of accreditation with autonomous management. In 1959, the educational institutions were shifted from Odessa to Zaporozhyand in the year of 1968, the institute was renamed as Zaporozhye Medical Institute.
In 1994, the institute received the status of State Medical University which provides higher education services offering courses in bachelor, master, specialists, postgraduate studies, internships, doctoral studies, pharmacists, etc.
It is one of the oldest & best medical higher educational institutions in Ukraine. For more than 40 years, it has gained authority and recognition by training foreign citizens from Europe, Latin America, USA, Canada, Asia, and Africa. More than 3500 specialists have got medical and pharmaceutical education and are working now in more than 100 countries of the world, taking up high positions in the public health systems of their countries.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN UKRAINE FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • Zaporozhye State Medical University is NMC approved and also recognized by WHO and many medical councils of other countries.
  • It offers an affordable fee structure for students.
  • The medical degree from Zaporozhye State Medical University is recognized worldwide. The students of Zaporozhye State Medical University can practice their medicine in all the recognized countries.
  • Zaporozhye State Medical University has highly qualified professors.
  • Highly professional and advanced technology is used to teach students.
