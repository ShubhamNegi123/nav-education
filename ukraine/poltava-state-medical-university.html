Poltava State Medical
University

Poltava Ukrainian Medical and Dental Academy started its operation in 1921 with faculty of Kharkiv Medical Academy. In 1967, the university is moved to the city of Poltava and named as Poltava State National Medical University.
In 1994, the Poltava Medical Dental University received the accreditation of Ukrainian Medical Dental Academy. About 4000 students are studying in this academy. Out of them, 1200 are the foreigners from 42 countries.Training is conducted for medical and dental education.
The Poltava State Medical and Dental University is one of the highly ranked University in Ukraine providing quality Medical and Dental Education for undergraduate students. From the year 1999 to 2009, academy published over 710 educational benefits receiving 106 patents of Ukraine and Russia.
Ukrainian Medical and Dental Academy (UMDA) has started teaching its students in accordance with the Bologna Agreement using credit modular system.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN UKRAINE FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • Poltava State Medical University provides MBBS education in English medium for the entire 6 years MBBS course in Ukraine.
  • The university have highly qualified teachers, trainers, faculties, lecturers and even the laboratory staff. Because of this, the university is able to provide quality education to students.
  • As compared with the other countries, Poltava State Medical University in Ukraine, provides low cost and affordable MBBS tuition fee to its international students.
  • The university is recognized from World Health Organization (WHO) and other medical organizations.
  • Students can appear for different licensing exams all over the globe like PLAB, NZREX, NEXT, USMLE and more.
  • To get admission in Poltava State Medical University, there are no entrance exams as such.
  • The University has direct admission procedure once the student meets the basic eligibility criteria.
  • There are no entrance exams or tests like ILETS/ TOEFL.
  • The admissions are done on First Come First Serve Basis, which is why, it is always better that you take admission faster.
  • Poltava State Medical University is also a part of bologna process which ensures high quality education and follows European style of education standard.
  • The Poltava State Medical University is recognized by medical organizations, hence is approved globally.
  • There are no donations, hidden charges while taking admission in this university
  • Poltava State Medical University also offers hostel facilities to its international students.
  • The hostels are at Poltava State Medical University which are nearby and are comfortable with no overcrowding as such.
