Ivano National Medical
University

In 1945, the Ivano Frankivsk National Medical University was founded. The university is located in Ivano Frankivsk city and it is the non-profit public medical institution.
The Ivano Frankivsk National Medical University is recognized by the World Health Organization (WHO), National Medical Commission (NMC), and the world directory of medical schools. It is the leading and one of the oldest medical universities in Ukraine.
It has a total of 12 hospitals and clinics and there are a total of 48 departments at the Ivano Frankivsk National Medical University.
Nearly, 4000 students from all over the world, like from countries like Australia, Africa, the USA, India, and other parts of Asia, study medicine at Ivano Frankivsk National Medical University. This university is the perfect destination for students who seek quality MBBS education at a reasonable price.
