North Sichuan Medical University was established in the year 1951 by Sichuan Provincial Department of Education. The university rests in the famous historical place Nanchang in Sichuan Province where you can find the mix of three kingdoms. North Sichuan Medical College consists of two campuses Shunqing and Gaoping which covers a wide range of area. The university is continuously working to improve the medical services and structure to enhance the best medical services. This is the reason why the university motto works on four pillars dedication, fraternity, seeking truth, and innovation.
