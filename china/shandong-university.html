shandong University

Shandong University was founded in 1901 and it is one of the oldest and prestigious universities in China. This university has got an approval as a “national key comprehensive university” by the Ministry of Education. By the Ministry of Education, it was listed among the 21 national key construction first-class universities in 2001. Shandong University, located in Jinan covers a total area of approximately 3.6 square kilometers.
Shandong University in Jinan consists of 30 colleges and a graduate school. It offers courses in 10 main academic disciplines namely philosophy, economics, history, natural sciences, engineering, law, literature, management, education and medicine. School of Medicine of Shandong University is one of the oldest medical schools in China.
The history of this school dates back to the Medical School of Cheeloo University founded in 1911. The Medical School hosts 27 preclinical and clinical departments, 12 teaching hospitals, 13 teaching labs, 6 affiliated hospitals, and 7 training hospitals. Shandong University also consists of one of the top nursing schools in China founded in 2000 whose undergraduate nursing program was started from 1985 among the first to restore higher nursing education.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN china FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • Shandong Medical University School of Medicine provides various extracurricular activities and alternative learning activities.
  • Out of these, some of the activities are enjoyed by most of the foreign students and domestic students as well as they like learning calligraphy, football, tai-chi, and other games and taking choir classes.
  • Students can also demonstrate the skills they have learned at events or activities organized by the university and there they can perform in front of an audience of classmates, faculty and friends.
  • Students are allotted with hostel facilities at the university where students get all the amenities.
  • At a very reasonable cost, breakfast, lunch, supper and all kinds of snacks are available for international students.
  • To keep students fit and active, the University has a well established Sports Ground for indoor and outdoor sports both in New & Old Campus.
  • The University has modern educational facilities which include the Computer Center, Network Teaching Laboratories, Multimedia Micro-teaching Laboratories, Audio-visual Educational Information Center, Artists Gallery, campus-net, Multimedia Classrooms, Music Hall, cable TV and broadcasting systems. So students can get all the best facilities and environment for teaching.
  • At this university, all highly qualified teachers teach MBBS courses, so as to make the teaching-learning experience a live procedure. This enhances practical capability and the content understanding of a student.
  • Students enjoy a multicultural environment at the university due to the presence of students from multiple countries
