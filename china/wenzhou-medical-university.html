Wenzhou medical University

In the year 1912, Wenzhou Medical University was founded in Hangzhou. In August 1958, the Zhejiang Medical College was relocated from Hangzhou to Wenzhou. It was originally named "Zhejiang Second Medical College" and was later named "Wenzhou Medical College”, and then renamed as “Wenzhou Medical University” in 2013.
Wenzhou Medical University enfolds internationalization in the course of its development. The university has established partnerships and associations with over 80 universities and institutions all across the globe, collaborating in scholar/student exchanges, joint research, joint education programs, etc. Wenzhou Medical University is one of the most robust and the largest schools for clinical medicine sciences in Zhejiang Province.
