Anhui Medical
University

Anhui Medical University China was founded in the year 1926. It is one of the recognized universities to study the MBBS course, situated in Anhui, China. AMU China has four main campus areas: the campus of Meishan Road Area, Feicui Road Area, the east campus (under construction), and the campus of Chaohu School of Clinical Medicine, covering an area of 1820 mu in total.
