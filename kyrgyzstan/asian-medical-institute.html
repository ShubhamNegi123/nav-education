Asian Medical Institute

Study MBBS at Asian Medical Institute at an affordable fee. Established in 2004, Asian Medical Institute is located in Kant, Kyrgyzstan. It is just 20 KM away from the capital Bishkek and the Manas International Airport is 40 KM away from the institute. It is named after S. Tentishev and is also known by the name S.Tentishev Asian Medical Institute.
Asian Medical Institute is an NMC (National Medical Commission) approved university which makes it trustworthy for Indian students. It is also recognized by the World Health Organization and other prestigious councils and organizations. There are 3 operating campuses of Asian Medical University. It is ranked among the top 10 medical universities in Kyrgyzstan and ranks 28th among all universities in Kyrgyzstan. The university has so much to offer for Indian students.
There are more than 2000 international students from 10 countries including India, Russia, Nepal, Germany, Pakistan, Kazakhstan are already pursuing medical studies from Asian Medical Institute and more than 15000 students have pursued their medical degrees from this university.
Asian Medical Institute offers Indian students special coaching for the preparation of the NMC screening test from the first year. Indian professors are allotted for the students to help them and teach them for the same. They also help students to update Indian medical developments and news. The teaching language is English which makes learning much easier.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN KYRGYSTAN FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • Asian Medical Institute has an affordable fee structure for Indian students.
  • It is the top-ranked University with quality education.
  • The medium of teaching at the Asian Medical Institute is English which makes Indian students understand better.
  • Asian medical institute is worldwide recognized and also NMC approved, so students can practice in India after completing a medical degree in the Asian Medical Institute.
  • Asian Medical Institute has minimum eligibility criteria for Indian students. Students need not qualify IELTS or TOEFL.
  • The institute has 8 hospitals reserved for the students to teach perfectly.
  • Asian Medical Institute has a modern campus.
  • Qualified and experienced staff from foreign and local.
