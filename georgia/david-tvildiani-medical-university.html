David Tvildiani Medical
University

David Tvildiani Medical University is one of the first choices of students among all medical institutes located in Tbilisi, Georgia. Established by David Tvildiani and Dr Dimitri Tvildiani in the year of 1989, the university is always a prime choice of worldwide medical aspirants for doing MBBS.
David Tvildiani Medical University aims to provide higher medical education to worldwide students along with affordable fee structure, modern infrastructure, basic amenities, accommodation facilities and also developed PhD, doctorate programs, nursing school and AIETI Medical School.
Currently, 200 graduates have passed from David Tvildiani Medical University and are working as physicians in USA and European Universities and Clinics.
The students who have graduated from David Tvildiani Medical University have successfully cleared the international exams like USMLE, PLAB, NMC. Some students are currently developing their skills at clinics and research centres of different countries, some students became proficient teachers and many students have earned their PhD degree from DTMU.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN GEORGIA FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • The medium of teaching for MBBS education is English and Georgian language.
  • Recognized by the WHO & NMC and has been verified and authorized by the Medical Examination Committees of the USA, different countries of Europe and Asia.
  • The MBBS tuition fee at David Tvildiani Medical University is low and affordable for students.
  • The students gain high-quality education from highly qualified professors, teachers and doctors at DTMU.
  • The easy documentation process for MBBS admission at DTMU.
  • The living expense is quite affordable and economical.
  • No need to appear for the university entrance exam.
  • David Tvildiani Medical University has no language barrier.
  • DTMU has a post-medical preparation program like USMLE.
