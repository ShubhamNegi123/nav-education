Grigol Robakidze
University

Grigol Robakidze University is a higher medical institution located in Tbilisi, Georgia and was established in 1992. The university was named in honour of Grigol Robakidze, who was a Georgian poet and thinker.
The Grigol Robakidze University is among the 5 higher educational institutions of Georgia In terms of student employment according to UNESCO International Institute of Education Management. The Grigol Robakidze University uses modern technologies including case methods, modelling methods, master classes, projects, discussions, interactive and presentation methods of delivering lectures etc. It provides highly qualified, dedicated and experienced professors and teachers and latest material-technical resources.
