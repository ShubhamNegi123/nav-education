ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN Armenia FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • A student can apply for internships in various hospitals in Armenia, after completing his/her first year of studies.
  • The graduate degree which students get in MBBS from Mkhitar Gosh University is valid in India and other countries.
  • Students can practice medicine in any part of the world once they qualify for screening tests of the National Medical Commission (NMC).
  • High-quality education in MBBS courses is ensured through the combination of modern medicine and practical training.
  • Connecting flights are easily available, if students want to travel to and fro from India to Armenia.
  • The university has approval from the National Medical Commission (NMC).
  • The Mkhitar Gosh university follows a curriculum similar to that of MBBS in India. Thus, Indian students find it easy to mix up in the new environment and the education system of Mkhitar Gosh University.
  • Language is not a barrier because English is the medium of instruction while studying MBBS in the Mkhitar Gosh university.
  • No need to clear IELTS or TOEFL for admission into Mkhitar Gosh University.
