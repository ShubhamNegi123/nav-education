Shahbuddin Medical
College

Shahabuddin Medical College is a well-known medical college in Bangladesh offering MBBS to all international students at an affordable cost. The college was founded in 2003 and is situated in the heart of the Dhaka City- Gulshan. The college is dully recognized by the by Bangladesh Medical & Dental Council and the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.
Shahabuddin Medical College is enlisted with WDOMS (World Directory of Medical Schools) and IMED (International Medical Education Directory).
The college has an affiliated hospital of 500 bedded, giving students a vast practical exposure due to the high inflow of patients. Medical graduates are eligible to practice their medical career in any part of the world after qualifying the FMGE.
The faculty members are highly experienced and qualified in their respective fields. At present, the college has students from various countries including India, Pakistan, Nepal, and other countries.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN BANGLADESH FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • The cost of studying MBBS at Shahabuddin Medical College is less which is affordable for many students.
  • The official language for teaching MBBS programs in the English language.
  • The students across the world will get a medical degree with global acceptance.
  • Availability of cutting edge modern technologies used in medical studies in Shahabuddin Medical College.
  • The Shahabuddin Medical College has an experienced faculty and staff strength to impart top-class education to young minds.
  • The Shahabuddin Medical College regularly conducts events, seminars and presentations etc.
  • The campus includes 12 storied college buildings and a hospital building holding all the attributes of modern architecture.
  • Shahabuddin Medical College has all modern facilities like Library, Lecture Halls, Conference rooms, CME unit, Common room, Prayer room, Canteen and one wing of the Female Hostel.
  • No need to appear for any English entrance exams like IELTS/TOEFL/PTE to enroll in MBBS course at Shahabuddin Medical College.
  • The local students of Bangladesh have a friendly relationship with Indian students during medical education.
  • Medical graduates are eligible to practice their medical career in any country of their choice after qualifying the FMGE.
  • Shahabuddin Medical College offers high-quality education at world standards and other latest facilities.
  • Apart from studies, Shahabuddin Medical College hosts academic cultural activities, festivals, games and sports facilities- football courts, volleyball courts, gymnasium and swimming pool.
