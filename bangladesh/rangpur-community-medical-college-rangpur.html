Rangpur Community Medical
College Rangpur

Rangpur Community Medical College is a prestigious Private Medical College located in Pirzabad, on the western fringes of Rangpur, Bangladesh. Rangpur Community Medical College is a leading and prestigious college in Bangladesh, affiliated with the University of Rajshahi and established in 2008 and situated at Medical East Gate, Health City Rd, Rangpur, Bangladesh.
Within a short period, they have achieved their committed target and served the best to their student as promised. Rangpur Community Medical College is proud to have students not only nationally but also internationally. Their 50% of total students are foreign students pursuing MBBS & BDS courses in their respective colleges.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN BANGLADESH FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • Rangpur Community Medical College provides MBBS to medical students from Bangladesh as well as international students who want to study medicine in foreign universities.
  • The total duration of the MBBS program at Rangpur Community Medical College in Bangladesh is 5 years, plus a one-year mandatory internship.
  • The Bangladesh Medical and Dental Council recognizes the degree.
  • The Rangpur Community Medical College campus is well connected by all modes of transportation and is only a short distance from excellent dining and shopping options.
  • The college has well-equipped residential hostels for students near the college, as well as a dining hall and provisions for group dining where healthy and nutritious food is served.
  • Bangladeshi cuisine is very similar to Indian cuisine, making it very easy for Indian students to adjust.
  • The disease pattern of patients in Bangladesh is also very similar to the disease pattern of patients in India. This is an added benefit for students
