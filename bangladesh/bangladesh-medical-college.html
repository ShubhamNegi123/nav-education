Bangladesh Medical
College Dhaka

Bangladesh Medical College is the oldest private medical institute to study MBBS in Bangladesh. Seats are reserved for Indian students in top MCI recognized medical colleges in Bangladesh. The college allows MBBS admission in total one hundred and thirty (130). Study MBBS in Bangladesh becomes an ideal location for Indian as well as other international students to study MBBS in Abroad.
The college was established in 1986 and recognized by Bangladesh Medical and Dental council. It has 500 beds attached hospital in Dhanmondi Campus with a fully air-conditioned operation theatre, patient examination, and treatment arrangements.
The college produces skillful medical students every year and aims at the development of the students by modern teaching techniques, activities, and opportunities to participate in scientific forums. Bangladesh Medical College admission process is based on merit and students require attempting NEET Exam and scoring good percentile.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN BANGLADESH FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • One of the top private medical colleges in Bangladesh for medical education.
  • Easy admission process for foreign country students.
  • Very low MBBS course fee as compared to some of the other countries
  • Easy adaptibility due to the similar climatic conditions like India.
  • Similar Indian culture makes it easier and faster to settle down.
  • No entrance examination required.
  • Internationally approved medical college and degree.
  • The classroom in private medical colleges in Bangladesh delivers a lecture in the English language that is easy to understand for international students.
  • Travel cost from India to Bangladesh is quite low and takes a few hour journeys.
