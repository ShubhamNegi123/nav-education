Popular Medical
College

Popular Medical College prides itself on the highly qualified medical teaching staff. Popular Medical College provides a healthy environment for students to interact and form a lasting relationship, which proves to be beneficial in the in long term for the MBBS aspirant.
Popular Medical College is recognized by the Medical Council of India and the World Health Organization. The MBBS course at Popular Medical College is of six years. The medium of instruction for all foreign students at Popular Medical College is English. The duration of internship at Popular Medical College is one year.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN BANGLADESH FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • One of the benefits of popular medical college is that it is very close to India.
  • The time duration of the MBBS course at Popular Medical College is only five years. Unlike some other MBBS abroad destinations, the aspirants get to start their career a year earlier.
  • The educational methods and syllabus are similar to those taught in india.
  • The studying cost at Popular Medical collegw is much less than that in any Indian Private college with almost the same facilities as those provided in good private colleges across India.
  • Also, the living cost in Bangladesh is lower than that in India.
  • Students need not go for any entrance exams like IELTS/TOEFL?PTE to get admissions in the MBBS course at Popular Medical College.
  • The local people of Bangladesh have a good relationship with the Indian students
