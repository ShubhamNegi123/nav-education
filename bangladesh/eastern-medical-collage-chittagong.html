Chittagong

It is affiliated with Chittagong University and Chittagong Medical University. It offers a five-year program that culminates in a Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) degree. All graduates must complete a one-year internship after graduation. The Bangladesh Medical and Dental Council recognizes the degree.
The Kabila campus is home to a 600-bed hospital. In October 2014, the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare set a cap on admission and tuition fees at private medical colleges at 1,990,000 Bangladeshi takas (US$25,750 in 2014). The college offers a five-year program approved by the Bangladesh Medical and Dental Council (BMDC) that leads to a Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) degree from Chittagong University of Chittagong Medical University. After passing the final professional examination, a one-year internship is required.
