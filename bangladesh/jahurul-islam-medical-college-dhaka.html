Jahurul Islam Medical
College Dhaka

Jahurul Islam Medical College was established by Jahurul Islam in the year 1992. Jahurul Islam medical college and hospital is one of the oldest institutes present in Bangladesh. The college is located in Bhagalpur, Kishoreganj District. This college is spread in the area of around 53 acres and is also the first college to be established in the rural area.
Jahurul Islam medical college is a private institute and offers a five-year MBBS course. The MBBS degree is recognised by the world health organization (WHO) and the National Medical Commission (NMC). The college provides the best education along with the best infrastructure to the students. The students who have graduated from this college are recognised all over the world. Jahurul Islam medical college is one of the best options for foreign students as it gives the best study environment.
