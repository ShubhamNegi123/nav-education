Crimean Federal University was established on August 4, 2014, and has elaborated renowned MBBS education in Russia. Since establishment, the university offers organizational, scientific and methodological aspects of the educational process related to MBBS in Russia.

Well-determined clinical departments of the university provide the necessary equipment and demonstration aids such as educational films, diagnostic inventory to meet the modern requirements.

The university has a highly qualified staff of 850 persons including the 99 doctors of science, professors, 350 candidates of science, 40 university professors, associate professors, 14 state prize laureates, 60 have honorary titles. Every year, around 3000+ international students study medicine at the Crimean Federal University.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN BANGLADESH FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • The university has well-organized student exchange programs and has a partnership with reputed medical universities in Europe and other parts of the world.
  • Indian students who are studying MBBS at Crimean Federal University will get ample clinical exposure from the government hospitals located in the city of Simferopol.
  • The large community of Indian students studying at the university proves that the university provides the holistic culture to the students from different parts of the country
  • Indian cuisine is available for the students who offer the best Indian food to foreign students.
  • The university holds accreditation from the Russian Government as well as the Ministry of Health and Education.
  • Indian students will get the opportunity to participate in research programs under the guidance of senior professors and teachers during MBBS in Simferopol, Russia.
