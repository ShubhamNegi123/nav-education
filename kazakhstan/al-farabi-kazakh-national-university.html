Altai State Medical University is one of the leading medical schools in Russia located in the West Siberian part of the country. The university conducts high-quality training programs with 25,000 leading teams of doctors and pharmaceutics for the regional healthcare system. The university stands for one of the central hubs for research, education and cultural exchange.
Since years, the university has produced around 30,000 graduates. Yearly, around 4000 students appear for medical programs at the university. Most of the students are from foreign countries like India, Iraq, Bangladesh, Germany, Kyrgyzstan etc. In 1991, the university established the faculty of international students.
Under the faculty, the university prepares a number of foreign students in various disciplines like General Medicine, Dentistry, Pediatrics and Pharmacy. The university has active participation in the implementation of scientific projects, clinical research and innovation related to science and technology.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN KAZAKHSTAN FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • Altai State Medical University pays individual attention to the safety of students.
  • There will be no entrance exam or any enrollment exam while applying for MBBS course at Altai State Medical University.
  • Cost of living and studying at university is easily affordable.
  • There is an ample number of opportunities are available after completion of the MBBS from ASMU.
  • Experienced staff and excellent aspects of teaching.
  • Affiliated hospitals for providing training and internship programs.
  • No donation and capitation fees.
  • Clinical exposure is provided to enhance the practical knowledge of students.
