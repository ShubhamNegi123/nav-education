Astana Medical
University

Astana Medical University is a higher education medical institution located in the city of Astana, the capital of Kazakhstan and was established as an academy in the year of 1997.
The university is the most popular medical university of Kazakhstan and always endeavours to introduce innovative technologies to the education system for enhancing the overall quality of medical education. Astana Medical University is the preferred choice of many students looking for low-cost medical education.
For taking admission in MBBS course the application forms are open from July and runs till August. Thus, it's very crucial to apply before 30th July every year as the visa process may be long at least for 2-3 months.
