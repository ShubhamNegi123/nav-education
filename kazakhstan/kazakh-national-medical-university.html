Kazakh National Medical
University

It is one of the top medical universities of Kazakhstan. The university was established in the rural area of Almaty city in the year 1930. The government of Kazakhstan gave the title of National university to Kazakh National Medical University in the year 2001 due to its best academic results.
The Kazakh National Medical University has got recognition from the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan. Number of courses such as bachelor, masters and doctorate programs are offered by this university for students.
The university has well equipped, modern infrastructure and the best medical instruments. There are about 1500 faculty members who are working at the Kazakh National Medical University and about 11,000 students are studying at this university. Many students from many different countries apply to get admission at the Kazakh National Medical University.

ADVANTAGES TO STUDY IN KAZAKHSTAN FOR INDIAN STUDENTS

  • It is the top university in Kazakhstan.
  • The Kazakhstan city is rich in natural and pure mineral resources.
  • As Kazakhstan is a best place in terms of education sector, it is a regional study hub for central Asia.
  • At Kazakh national medical university, one semester contains 5 months.
  • The university offers its curriculum in English medium.
  • The fees are low compared to other universities.
  • The university has highly qualified teaching faculty and thus the quality of education is the best.
  • University has a very large and nice campus which is the largest in the country.
