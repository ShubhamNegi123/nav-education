  • The living expenses for international students in Kazakhstan are low.
  • Many of the top medical colleges of Kazakhstan have their own accommodations as the number of international students is high.

  • Kazakhstan medical colleges have many student-exchange programs which help students get a holistic experience.
  • More than 5,000 Indian students have enrolled in MBBS Kazakhstan in the MCI approved medical colleges so far.
